What is Threat Fest?

Threat Fest is a two night DIY event made up of some of Nashville's best local bands, as well as several regional touring acts. The festival was created to bring together the different communities of Middle Tennessee in an effort to celebrate the immense talent that it has to offer. Ultimately, Threat Fest is about encouraging one another to pursue their passions through art. If this is something you're interested in being a part of, you're in the right place.
