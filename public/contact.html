FAQ

Where do I park?

Parking is available across from the venue next to the train depot. Additional parking can be found across from Carpe Cafe.

Can I bring my dog/cat/bird of prey?

No animals are allowed inside venue, but you are more than welcome to have them outside of venue.

Is this 18 and up?

Threat Fest is all ages.

Can I bring drugs and alcohol?

No drugs or alcohol permitted. No weapons either.

Can I be a total jerk?

Be respectful of the space and everyone in it. Threat Fest is a welcoming environment to all, and should not be messed up because of the actions of jerks.

Can I drive my car through the building?

Please don't. But if you do, have a good lawyer.
